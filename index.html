My Journey

I'm building a world where work doesn't steal your whole life. After burning out in tech, I started talking online about what work was doing to us: the long hours, the quiet gaslighting, the feeling that your worth depends on your productivity.

I didn't expect it to resonate the way it did, but it sparked a global conversation around lazy girl jobs, quiet quitting, and what it really means to have balance. Now, I create content that helps people unlearn hustle culture, automate their 9 to 5 with AI, and build careers that actually fit their lives.

I'm the founder of Ms. Anti Work, a TedX speaker, and a contributor for Entrepreneur and HubSpot. My work has been featured by The New York Times, BBC, The Wall Street Journal, and 60 Minutes Australia, and my upcoming book You Are Not Your Job will be published by Simon & Schuster.
